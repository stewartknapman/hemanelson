Safety Disclaimer

We hit each other with swords. If you want to join us there is a high chance that you will also get hit by a sword. We occasionally leave with badges bruises. But, in all seriousness safety is extreamly important to us: we ensure that everyone has the correct safety gear before training and that no one is pushed to a level that they feel uncomfortable with.
