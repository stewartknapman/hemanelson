We are a small group of enthusiasts located in Nelson, New Zealand, who study Historical European Martial Arts—also known as HEMA.

What is… “HEMA”?

What disciplines do you study?

Currently we mainly study 14th century German longsword in the style of Johannes Liechtenauer. You can read more about Liechtenauer on Wiktenauer.

Safety Disclaimer

We hit each other with swords. If you want to join us there is a high chance that you will also get hit by a sword. We occasionally leave with badges bruises. But in all seriousness safety is extreamly important to us: we ensure that everyone has the correct safety gear before training and that no one is pushed to a level that they feel uncomfortable at.
