HEMA Nelson—Club Rules

General rules and safety

  1. Listen to and follow all safety rules and instructions set up by instructors.
  2. If you see anything unsafe or that you are unsure about, immediately advise an instructor.
  3. Because of the full contact nature of the classes all students 16â€“18 must have a parent or guardian present for the first 12 consecutive classes. Once this trial period is complete and if agreed upon by all parties then the student my attend classes unsupervised.
  4. Because of the full contact nature of the classes you may incur some bruising. We do our best to ensure everyone has the correct level of safety gear before commencing paired drills or sparring.
  5. All instructors hold at least a basic level of and up to date first aid training.

Gear requirements

  1. Please wear clothing and footwear that is suitable for moderate to strenuous physical exercise.
  2. Basic training weapons will be supplied by the club. However students are encouraged to purchase their own specialised or advanced training weapons as they progress.
  3. Due to health and safety reasons the club is unable to provide or loan safety gear (gloves and masks).
  4. All students must own or have personal access to the minimum required safety gear (suitable gloves and mask set) before they can engage in (medium or higher intensity) paired/contact drills or (light) sparring.
  5. The minimum required safety gear for sparring with heavy synthetic or steel weapons is: suitable gloves and mask; padded jacket or gambeson; neck or throat protection. In addition to this students are encouraged to also wear arm/elbow and shin/knee and upper leg protection.
  6. When purchasing new gear please check with an instructor if it is considered safe for training.
  7. Some gear may be able to be purchased directly from the club, or the club may be able to facilitate group orders if there is enough interest.
  8. Bare feet is not permitted at anytime.

Fees and payment

  1. Class fees are $15/student per lesson.
  2. All fees can be made in cash to the instructor or via Internet banking before the end of class. Please speak to an instructor to get the club bank account details.
  3. Some gear may be purchased through the club. Payment plans can be discussed when purchasing gear.

Club communication

  1. The club email address is hello@hemaneslon.com
  2. Internal club announcements and discussions are made via our closed Facebook group. Please speak to an instructor for more information on how to get access to this.
  3. You can message instructors at anytime via: Messenger, email, text message, etc. Please speak to an instructor to get their contact details.

Code of conduct

  1. The safety and enjoyment of all our members is incredibly important to us.
  2. Please be considerate and respectful of other peoples views, beliefs, personal space, and levels of fitness or ability.
  3. We will not tolerate racist, sexist, misogynistic, or other derogatory comments or actions, whether directed at another member or not.
  4. If you feel anyone has made inappropriate comments or actions towards you, please notify an instructor immediately.
  5. If you see anyone making inappropriate comments or actions towards someone else, please notify an instructor immediately.
  6. If anyone is found to have breached the club rules or code of conduct they will be removed from both the class and the premises and will not be allowed to return to the club or participate in any of its activities until further action has been taken by the club council as stated in the clubs constitution. In this situation any club fees or payments made are forfeit; any outstanding fees or payments will still be required to be paid to the club until the remaining balance has been cleared.
